Our Story

A Celebration of Flame, Craft & Community

Born from a reverence for open-flame cooking and the rich culinary heritage of the American South, Rondeau & Co. opened its doors in Nashville's historic Germantown district in 2018. Founded by Chef Marcus Calloway and restaurateur Elena Voss, our restaurant is a testament to the belief that extraordinary food begins with extraordinary ingredients and the elemental power of fire.

Every dish on our menu tells a story — of pastures we partner with, seasons we honor, and traditions we carry forward. From the hand-selected hardwoods that fuel our custom-built hearth to the rare vintages that fill our cellar, every detail is curated with intention.

Executive Chef

Marcus Calloway

With over two decades of culinary mastery spanning three continents, Chef Marcus Calloway brings a rare depth of experience to Rondeau & Co. Trained under Michelin-starred mentors in Lyon and Tokyo before returning to his Southern roots, Marcus has developed a deeply personal approach to live-fire cooking that blends classical French technique with the bold, honest flavors of American heartland cuisine.

His philosophy is simple: honor the ingredient, respect the flame, and never stop learning. Under his leadership, Rondeau & Co. has earned recognition from James Beard, Food & Wine, and Nashville Scene as one of the South's most compelling dining destinations.
